Heats & head starts
How head starts work: the leading team leaves first. Every other team's start is delayed by exactly how far behind they are. On Total time, that gap is the running total across all finished legs — so whoever crosses the line first in the final leg is your overall champion. On Previous leg only, the gap is just from the leg that came before.
